I love traveling to National Parks.

One of my favorite places to travel is Arches National Park!

  1. Hiking
    • Variety of hiking trails
    • Trails cater to different skill levels
    • Close by to other National Parks
  2. Scenic Views
    • Offers wideopen panoramic views
    • The red rock emphasizes sunrise and sunset
    • The natural arches aren't seen very many other places
    • Great for photography
  3. Stargazing Galore
    • Designated International Dark Sky Park
    • Away from light pollution from city lights
    • Stable rocks to set up a camera for star photography
